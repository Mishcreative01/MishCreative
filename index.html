Mish! Creative is a design studio owned by graphic artist Gregg Wood. The company began in 2006 when the job market was in the dumps, and Gregg was a fresh college graduate who was eager to start a career using his degree in graphic design and visual communications.

Our projects started with logo and promotional graphics for Philadephia based small businesses. In the 13 years since our first project our team has worked with a variety of clients across the globe. We’ve designed for businesses from New York City to Los Angeles, and have crossed borders to meet the needs of clients in Canada, Australia and Europe.

The Mish! Creative team is made up of innovative minds with the ability to represent you by using inventive and productive graphic design solutions that will catch the attention of your audience.

Mish! Creative’s goal is to create quality design solutions by using a mix of current design trends, creativity, technology and a talented team to help us achieve results.
